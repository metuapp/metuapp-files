Sevgili Ogrencilerimiz,

A4 Kapisinda yurutulen altyapi ve asfalt yenileme calismalari nedeniyle, 11 Eylul 2025 Persembe gunu saat 06.00'dan itibaren kapi arac trafigine tamamen kapatilacak; 12 Eylul Cuma gunu yalnizca arac girisine izin verilecek, cikislar ise diger kapilardan saglanacaktir. 13 ve 14 Eylul tarihlerinde ise A4 Kapisindan arac giris ve cikislari kontrollu olarak yapilacaktir.

Calismalar suresince gostereceginiz anlayis ve is birligi icin tesekkur eder, bilgilerinize sunariz.

Saygilarimizla,
Yapi Isleri ve Teknik Daire Baskanligi

----------------------------------------
Dear Students,
 
Due to ongoing infrastructure and asphalt renovation works at Gate A4, the gate will be completely closed to vehicle traffic starting from 06:00 on Thursday, September 11, 2025. On Friday, September 12, only vehicle entry will be permitted through this gate, while exits must be made through alternative gates. On September 13 and 14, vehicle entry and exit at Gate A4 will be allowed in a controlled manner.

We thank you for your understanding and cooperation during the course of the works and respectfully share this information for your attention.
 
Kind regards,
Division of Construction and Maintenance
